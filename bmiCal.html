_BMI CALCULATOR_








_ A healthy BMI ranges between 19 and 25 _

Instructions:

Body mass index (BMI) is a value derived from the mass (weight) and height of a person. The BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is expressed in units of kg/m2, resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres.

Basic Categories:

  • Underweight (Severe thinness) ⇢ The people with a BMI below 16.0 are underweight; severely lean if below 16
  • Underweight (Moderate thinness) ⇢ The people with a BMI in between 16.0 – 16.9 are moderately underweight.
  • Underweight (Mild thinness) ⇢ The people with a BMI in between 17.0 – 18.4 are mildly underweight.
  • Normal range ⇢ The people with a BMI in between 18.5 – 24.9 are normal; they're in the healthy weight range.
  • Overweight (Pre-obese) ⇢ The people with a BMI in between 25.0 – 29.9 are overweight; they've Waist Circumference, and Associated Disease Risks
  • Obese (Class I) ⇢ The people with a BMI in between 30.0 – 34.9 are in the classification of Overweight and Obesity by BMI
  • Obese (Class II) ⇢ The people with a BMI in between 35.0 – 39.9 are in Class 2 (moderate-risk) obesity.
  • Obese (Class III) ⇢ The people with a BMI above 40 are in Class 3 morbid obesity.
